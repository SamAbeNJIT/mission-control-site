Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your Data Stays Local

Mission Control is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your data is stored locally on your Mac and is not uploaded to our servers. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal information, calendar events, emails, or tasks.

Google Calendar & Gmail Access

Mission Control requests access to your Google Calendar and Gmail solely to provide you with the app's core functionality:

  • Reading your calendar events to help you plan your day
  • Creating and updating calendar events on your behalf
  • Reading emails to help you stay on top of communications

This access is used exclusively for your benefit within the app. Your Google data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

No Data Selling or Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties. Your information is yours alone.

No Analytics or Tracking

Mission Control does not include any analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry. We do not monitor your usage of the app or collect behavioral data.
